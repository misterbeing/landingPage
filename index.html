What New Features were added to JSL’s Daily Operations

FastSteel automated end-to-end weighment process and enabled tracking from the central control room. The Computer Vision-based solution enabled automated RFID Allocation, ANPR, multiple weigh-bridges monitoring, real-time weighment and weighing cycle insights generation, manual weighment shutdown, and real-time alerts generation.

FastSteel’s Development and Execution Process

Starting with the UI design, our developers used AngularJS to develop the FrontEnd and NodeJS, MongoDB, and Redis for the BackEnd. Furthermore, we also employed IoT and AI as well as components like Raspberry Pi, Sensors, and Custom Circuit Development.

What did JSL Acquire with our Unmanned Weighbridge App?

FastSteel has considerably increased efficiency and productivity while reducing 28% of operational costs and time for each weighing from 3.5 minutes to 40 seconds. Hence, JSL has complete transparency of each weighing cycle, ensuring complete security against theft.
